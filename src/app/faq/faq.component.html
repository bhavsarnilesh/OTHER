Frequently Asked Questions


1. How long does COVID-19 Live on Surfaces?
Ans. 6 hours - 9 days on surfaces
2. What is the difference between a virus and bacteria? Ans. Bacteria are self-contained, have cell walls, and can survive and replicate on their own. Viruses are DNA molecules that may be naked or encapsulated and require a host to replicate. They cannot be treated with antibiotics and require a vaccine
3. What is the biggest issue with this COVID-19 virus? Ans. High secondary infection rate, Rapid spread (more rapid than SARS or MERS), Fatality rate (2-3%)
4. Is ultraviolet light effective against COVID-19? Ans. Ultraviolet light destroys DNA of viruses, bacteria, and fungi
